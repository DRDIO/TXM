Explanation of Member Rankings
This summarizes member activity using an additive formula. Values such as Bootleg Views are estimated since there were no existing records of the values.

These issues will be ammended as more features are added to TXM.com and the weights for the formula are tweaked the better portray member's fame, status, and respect in the community.

Future ideas include the ability for members to rank their peers, and additional weights will be added for blogs and advertising campaigns as they are released.
